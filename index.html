Biography

Starting out in 2010 the main goal of the band was to write and play party-oriented punk rock, drink beer and just have fun with it. With this philosophy the boys in the band agreed upon the name The Friday Prophets. The first song that was written was named Punk Rock Flu which lived up to the original philosophy but as more songs came along the artistic and creative scope broaden. Fourteen songs later the band had finished their first full-length album Bits & Pieces which was released in November 2013. This album has the sound of that fast, melodic and loud 90's punk rock, when Green Day made Dookie, NOFX released Punk in Drublic and The Offspring dropped Smash. The Friday Prophets music is melody driven and the lyrics scrutinizes any aspect of life The bands influences comes in many shapes and forms from everything from books, articles, music, relationships, self-analyzing and more. The bands influences stem a lot from 90's and contemporary punk rock scene. Cited bands are Against Me!, Bad Religion, The Bouncing Souls, The Descendents, Green Day, No Use For a Name and NOFX. The guys in the band loves playing shows, seeing new places and meeting new faces. And beer of course.
